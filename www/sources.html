Youth Poverty Dataset

The dataset about poverty comes from this site. The raw data was collected by "U.S. Census Bureau's Small Area Income and Poverty Estimates (SAIPE) program". Data are available in 1989, 1993, 1995-2017.

Youth Tobacco Dataset

The dataset about youth smoking comes from this site. . It was collected by State Tobacco Activities Tracking and Evaluation (STATE) System, and then published on Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC). Data are available from 1999 to 2017.
